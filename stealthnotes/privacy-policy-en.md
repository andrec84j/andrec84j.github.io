# Privacy Policy and Copyright Terms - Stealth Notes

**Last Updated:** May 21, 2026

**Website:** [https://andrec84j.github.io/](https://andrec84j.github.io/)

**Stealth Notes** ("we", "our", or "app") is an ultra-secret notepad, secure screenshot capture, video recording, document management, and password vault application, built with a total focus on privacy and local-first architecture. This Privacy Policy and Copyright Terms describe how we handle your data and the terms under which the software is licensed.

---

## PART 1 - PRIVACY POLICY

### 1. Data Collection and Storage

#### 1.1 Local-First Architecture (Encrypted Vault)
Stealth Notes is a **100% Local-First** application. This means:
*   All your text notes, floating notes (Post-its), screenshot captures, video recordings, imported documents, and passwords are saved and stored **exclusively on your own device**.
*   To ensure maximum security, all your confidential data is saved inside an encrypted, secure local vault file (`vault.enc`).
*   **We do not send** your files, media, text, or passwords to our servers. We do not operate any cloud servers to store your personal content.
*   **No Telemetry:** The application does not collect, track, or send telemetry, analytics, or usage logs to the developers. Everything is processed entirely on your local machine.

#### 1.2 AI (Artificial Intelligence) and OCR Features
To offer cutting-edge features such as text recognition and AI-powered note editing:
*   **Local OCR (Optical Character Recognition):** This process runs entirely locally on your device utilizing native Windows APIs. Your captured images are never sent over the internet for OCR processing.
*   **Optional AI Services:** The application allows integration with Artificial Intelligence APIs (OpenAI, Google Gemini, Anthropic Claude) to assist you in writing, summarizing, or translating your notes.
*   **User API Keys:** Using AI is entirely optional and requires you to input your own API keys (tokens). These keys are stored securely on your computer in an encrypted format.
*   **AI Data Transmission:** When you trigger an AI feature, only the text or images strictly necessary for that specific request are transmitted directly from your computer to the chosen AI provider. No data passes through any third-party or developer servers. The usage of these keys and data is subject to the respective privacy policies of those providers.

---

### 2. Device Permissions

Stealth Notes requires the following system permissions to operate correctly:
*   **Screen Capture & Recording:** Required to take screenshots of your desktop and record video clips when requested by you.
*   **Audio Recording / Microphone (Optional):** Required only if you choose to include microphone audio in your local video recordings.
*   **Run at Startup (Startup):** Allows the application to optionally start automatically when Windows boots. This option can be easily enabled or disabled at any time through the system tray icon menu or the application settings.
*   **File System Access:** Used to read and write your exported media, application configurations, and your encrypted vault file.

---

### 3. Stealth Mode (Window Capture Protection)
The application features a unique **Stealth Mode**, which leverages low-level Windows APIs to hide the main application windows and floating Post-it notes from screenshots or screen recordings executed by external software. This prevents third parties from accidentally capturing or snooping on your confidential notes while you share or record your screen.

---

### 4. Data Sharing with Third Parties
Since the application functions completely locally, **we do not sell, rent, share, or disclose** any of your personal information. You maintain absolute and exclusive control over your security keys and generated files.
